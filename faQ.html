Frequently Asked Questions

What courses are offered in the college?

The college offers Computer Science, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics programs.

Is attendance compulsory?

Yes, students must maintain the minimum attendance percentage as per university rules.

How can students contact faculty members?

Students can contact faculty through official college email or during office hours.

Does the college provide placement support?

Yes, the college has a placement cell that helps students with internships and job opportunities.

Are extracurricular activities available?

Yes, students can join technical clubs, cultural groups, sports teams and social activities.
